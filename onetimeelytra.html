OneTimeElytra 1.1

Ever wanted to fly, but couldn't get an elytra? Now you can craft items for a one-time-elytra!

Native Minecraft Version: 1.19
Tested Minecraft Versions: 1.19

Discribtion:

Let's face it, elytras are annoying to get. Well, with this plugin, you can just craft items to glide.
Simply press rightclick with it and you'll start gliding!

Pro Tip: Hold a firework rocket in your offhand and you will be boosted instantly!
