Advanced Biomes

This mod adds new biomes, mobs and structures to minecraft!

Native Minecraft Version: 1.18.2
Tested Minecraft Versions: 1.18.2

Description:

- You can find the Biomes: Volcano, Heaven, Bluemushroomforest, Abandoned city, Glowingforest and The Wilds
- New mobs: Demon, Angel, Bluemushroomcow, Bluemushroomcow (angry), Box, Survivor, Possessed villager and pirat skeleton
- New structures: Volcano Fortress, Volcano, Heaven Fortress, Bluemushroom home, dead tower, dead tower mini, dead tower 2 and pirat ship
